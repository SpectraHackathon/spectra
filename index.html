About Spectra

Spectra is the first all足 ladies hackathon in the west coast taking place in the Bay Area! This is a unique event to bring together high schoolers, college students, and women in technology from all over.

The goal is to provide a safe and inviting environment for women to experiment with technology without any sort of apprehension. Free from gender stereotypes, this all足 ladies hackathon will give our attendees the space to breathe and work comfortably in tech to hone their skills. The overarching goal would be to make them comfortable enough with their own skills before taking on the male足dominated tech industry.

We hope to gather over 400 beautiful ladies and supporters to come together on July 22nd to July 24th for a 36 hour hackathon to learn new skills, inspire each other, and build long足lasting connections! We want to give more ladies the opportunity to make an impact in Computer Science.
